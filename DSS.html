DSS is where the money shows up.

Distributor Support Service holds your Sales Report — every sale, what you're owed, when you've been paid, and the points you've stacked along the way.

Here's how to pull it, one step at a time.

01Log In

Same door as always. Log in to the Distributor Support Portal with your Distributor ID and password.

02Open Distributor Support Service

Open the menu in the top-left. In your Shortcuts, click Distributor Support Service — the green one. That's DSS, and it opens your Sales Report.

03Open Sales Report

The Distributor Support Service menu opens with two buttons. Click Sales Report. (The 6A Group Sales button is only for 6A2 and above.)

04Set Your Dates and Search

Set the Sales Date range you want to track. Click the date field, pick your start and end dates from the calendar, then click Search. Nothing fills in until you hit Search.

05Read Your Results and Total

Your report fills in below — every sale with its date, product, amount, and whether it's been paid. Scroll to the last page, and the Total in the middle bottom is your accumulated number.

Tip — The Paid column tells the story. A date means you've been paid. Blank means you're still waiting.
