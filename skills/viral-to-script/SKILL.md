# Viral to Script — Team True North

Turn a SortFeed batch into shoot-ready reel scripts, up to 10 per run. The full training guide lives here — read it once before first use, and point the team member to it when a rule needs more context:

https://agency-true-north.github.io/team-true-north/Viral%20to%20Script%20System.html

Do not try to scrape or fetch the videos. The user's notes are the input. For each video they may give: the hook, the format/structure, the view count, the source page. If a video's mechanic isn't clear from the notes, ask plainly — don't guess.

## The one rule that outranks everything

**Model the format and hook mechanics. Never the topic.**

The creative is the targeting — Instagram serves a reel to people who resemble who it sounds like. Borrow a topic and you inherit its audience. Kill on sight, no matter the view count:

- Money-hype dialect — "make money online," "side hustle," "$X a day," "passive income" as a hype phrase, earnings-screenshot energy
- Marketer vocabulary — affiliate links, funnels, agencies, day-trading, crypto, "scale to six figures"
- Hustle-bro framing — grind worship, 4 a.m. flexing
- Bait mechanics — "comment WORD below," "DM me for info," follow-for-part-two, emoji bait
- Income numbers of any kind — none, ever, in public content

If a viral's mechanic can't leave its topic behind, skip it and say why. A skipped viral costs nothing; the wrong audience costs the right one.

## The process (per video)

1. **Extract the transferable mechanic only** — hook type, structure, retention device, emotional driver. One line each. If the mechanic can't be named without the topic, it hasn't been found yet.
2. **Run the kill list above.**
3. **Re-anchor on the woman we serve** — 30s–50s, the markers of success and none of the ease, evening-scrolling, problem-aware. Anchor in: the choice (she gets to choose everything), health / money / relationships / freedom, systems over grinding, Build a Bigger Table. Lead from certainty — never name the doubt, not even to answer it.
4. **Run the Kallaway 8**, layered on the voice: curiosity & contrast, speed to value (3 seconds earns the next 10), value density, clarity (one idea per beat, say the key point twice — once precise, once with a picture), absorption, anticipation, emotional resonance (pick the one emotion, choose every word for it), rhythm (short beats, read aloud before filming).
5. **Run the voice check** (below) on every finished script.

## Two formats — tag every script

Roughly 70/30. Default is VO; at most one TH per weekly batch.

- **VO — voiceover over life footage** (the workhorse). Real footage from the user's actual day, shot in passing on a phone. If the shot list needs lighting, a hair decision, or a scheduled shoot, it's written wrong.
- **TH — talking head.** Only for messages that lose weight without a face: contrarian beliefs, hard truths, the named frameworks.

## Every script includes

- Format tag (VO or TH)
- Modeled from — source page + mechanic, one line each
- Hook, first 3 seconds — spoken version and on-screen version, written separately
- Numbered beats, one idea per beat
- On-screen text cues (beat → text)
- B-roll shot list (VO only) — one real-life shot per beat
- Caption — short, second person, opens a door
- CTA — or a deliberate decision to run without one
- A pre-filled tracker row

## CTA rules

Most scripts carry no hard CTA — recognition is the job. When one earns its place, it points calmly to the **True North Masterclass** ("thirty minutes, linked in bio, when you're ready"). Never "DM me," never a countdown, never a pitch. Naming: the **True North Masterclass** is the class; the **True North Method** is the app — don't swap them.

## The voice check (every script, one miss sends it back)

- Second person all the way through — the picture lives in her eyes
- No exclamation marks, no all-caps lines, no emoji bait
- None of these words: manifest, transform, unlock, empower, elevate, journey, abundance, boss babe, side hustle, "this opportunity"
- No guilt as a motivator — especially motherhood, health, or success
- No income numbers — that conversation lives inside the masterclass
- The doubt is never named — stand in certainty and choice
- Read aloud once; if it sounds like a funnel, kill it and restart from the mechanic

## The tracker

Keep one running table, one row per script, forever — a markdown file, doc, or sheet the user chooses. Columns:

| Date | Source Page | Source Link | Views | Mechanic Modeled | Format | Status | Posted | Performance |

Status moves scripted → shot → posted. When the user reports a post or its numbers, update the row — the Performance column is how they learn which mechanics work on *their* audience.

## Before delivering

Present the scripts, then close with the calls only the user can make: which to shoot, which to kill, and the one TH pick of the week.
