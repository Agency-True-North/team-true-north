DGS is your map.

Distributor Genealogy Solution shows your whole team — who's where, who's growing, and what's happening underneath you.

Here's how to get in and read it, one step at a time.

01Open the Portal Menu

Start in the Distributor Support Portal at information.enagic.com. The menu lives in the top-left corner.

02Open Distributor Genealogy Solution

The menu slides open with your shortcuts. One of them is Distributor Genealogy Solution — that's DGS. Click it.

03Your Genealogy Tree

This is your tree. Every box is a distributor under you. The Summary line up top counts your ranks. To look anyone up, type their Distributor ID in the search box.

Tip — Each colored box is one person, and the color is their rank. The Legend (Step 5) tells you what every color means.

04Read a Distributor's Details

Turn the Menu toggle on in the top-right, then click any box. The panel on the right fills in with that person's details.

Tip — This is how you check a team member's rank and recent sales in a glance.

05Read the Legend

Open the Legend tab in the right panel. It decodes everything — what each color means, and what the three numbers on a box stand for.

Tip — Once the colors and numbers click, you can read your whole team in a glance.

That's DGS.

Get in through the Portal, open your tree, click a box for details, and lean on the Legend until the colors make sense.

It's the clearest picture you'll get of your team.
