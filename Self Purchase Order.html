How to place a self-purchase order in Enagic

This is how you buy for yourself through Enagic.

Your own products, supplies, or accessories — start to finish, six steps.

Take it slow and double-check your ID along the way. Once an order is processed, it can't be changed.

01Log In With Your ID

Sign in to the Distributor Support Portal with the ID you want to place the order under. Use the right ID — this is the account the order gets credited to.

02Open the Enagic Store

Open the menu in the top-left. On the left, click Enagic Store.

03Start a Self Purchase Order

Under "Buying Products for Yourself," check the ID is the one you want and your Line Rank is right. Then click Self Purchase Orders.

Tip — No changes are allowed once the order is processed. Get the ID and rank right before you go on.

04Confirm Your Details and Continue

Review your details — Buyer, Enroller, Distributor, Line Rank, and email. If everything's right, click Proceed to EOS in the top-right corner.

05Confirm to Enter the Store

A small box pops up asking if you're sure you want to go to the Enagic Store. Click OK.

06Shop the Store

You're in the Enagic Store. Pick what you need from the top menu — a product, supplies, accessories, and more — then check out.

Tip — Your Enroller, Sponsor, and Line Rank show at the top of the store. Confirm they're right before you buy.

That's a self purchase order.

Log in with the right ID, open the Enagic Store, start the self purchase, confirm your details, and shop.

Slow is smooth — double-check the ID and rank, because they lock once it's processed.
